What’s the Deal?: Top 10 Netflix Originals of the Year

Published June 2025

Explore the most talked about Netflix originals that have defined this year's streaming experience.

Top 10 Netflix Originals of the Year

1. Squid Game

This South Korean thriller took the world by storm with its gripping storyline and intense character arcs. The show explores themes of survival and morality, drawing viewers into a dark yet captivating narrative that keeps them on the edge of their seats.

2. Stranger Things 4

The much-anticipated fourth season of Stranger Things delivered nostalgia, suspense, and exceptional performances. Fans were thrilled to see the familiar faces of the kids from Hawkins, Indiana, as they faced new supernatural threats.

3. Bridgerton Season 2

This period drama continues to charm audiences with its lavish productions and enchanting romances. Season two focuses on new characters and love stories, maintaining the show's signature blend of intrigue and scandal.

4. Ozark Season 4

The final season of Ozark delivered heart-stopping moments and shocking twists. As the Byrde family navigates their dangerous lifestyle, viewers are left questioning their morals and rooting for their survival.

5. The Crown Season 5

This historical drama continues to captivate with its portrayal of the British royal family. Season five delves into the tumultuous 1990s, showcasing the personal and public challenges faced by Queen Elizabeth II and her family.

6. Wednesday

Tim Burton's take on the Addams Family’s daughter brought a fresh perspective to the beloved characters. Combining horror with comedy, Wednesday has quickly become a fan favorite, particularly for younger audiences.

7. Heartstopper

This coming-of-age series is a tender exploration of young love and friendship. With its diverse representation and heartwarming narrative, Heartstopper has captured the hearts of viewers around the globe.

8. Money Heist: Korea - Joint Economic Area

A thrilling remake of the widely popular Spanish series, this adaptation brings a unique cultural spin while maintaining the original's high-stakes drama. Fans of action-packed narratives will find themselves on lockdown binge-watching this series.

9. Hellbound

This unique blend of fantasy and horror explores societal issues through a supernatural lens. The series tackles moral dilemmas and the reactions of a world gripped by fear and prophecy, resonating with many viewers.

10. Red Notice

An action-comedy featuring A-list celebrities like Dwayne Johnson, Ryan Reynolds, and Gal Gadot. The film combines humor and high-stakes heists in a visually stunning package that kept viewers entertained from start to finish.

As streaming continues to evolve, these Netflix originals set a new standard for storytelling and production quality this year. With a diverse range of genres and themes, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Don’t miss out on these captivating shows and movies!
