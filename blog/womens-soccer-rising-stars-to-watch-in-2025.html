What’s the Deal?: Women’s Soccer: Rising Stars to Watch in 2025

Published June 2025

As women's soccer continues to grow both in popularity and skill level worldwide, an exciting new generation of players is emerging. With the next major international tournaments and leagues on the horizon, it’s important to keep an eye on these young talents who could redefine the future of the game. Here are some of the rising stars of women’s soccer to look out for in 2025.

Women’s Soccer: Rising Stars to Watch in 2025

1. Savannah DeMelo

At just 24 years old, Savannah DeMelo has already made waves in the professional soccer scene with her impressive performances. A midfielder known for her excellent vision and passing ability, DeMelo has established herself at the club level in the NWSL with her team. Her capability to control the pace of the game and her knack for setting up goal-scoring opportunities makes her a player to watch. As she gains experience, her potential to impact the U.S. Women’s National Team is immeasurable.

2. Mia Fishel

Mia Fishel is quickly making a name for herself as a forward capable of changing the dynamics of a match. The 23-year-old has shown remarkable talent in both college soccer and her professional engagements. Fishel's agility, combined with her finishing skills, has allowed her to shine in crucial moments. With her relentless work ethic and dedication, her rise may well coincide with increased opportunities on the international stage. Expect her to play a vital role in the World Cup and beyond.

3. CODIE LEE

A versatile defender, Codie Lee has proven to be more than just a strong presence on the back line. At 21, Lee is already recognized for her leadership qualities and tactical intelligence. She has consistently impressed at the youth international levels, making her an essential player to consider for upcoming senior teams. As women’s soccer evolves, her capability to transition from defense to attack will be an asset that clubs and national teams will covet.

4. Kelsey Turnbow

Kelsey Turnbow is a standout forward with a keen eye for the goal. At 25, she has already been pivotal to her NWSL team, showcasing her ability to create scoring opportunities and finish them with precision. Her relentless drive and competitive spirit are infectious, inspiring teammates and fans alike. As she finds her rhythm on the international stage, Turnbow may become one of the most exciting talents to watch as she aims for a spot on the national squad leading up to the next World Cup.

5. Alyssa Thompson

Alyssa Thompson, a prodigious talent who broke into the professional scene early, continues to demonstrate her potential as a game-changer. With speed that can outpace defenders and a natural ability to read the game, Thompson is a forward who poses a constant threat. The excitement surrounding her is palpable, and as she matures, many believe she could become a household name in the world of women's soccer by 2025.

Conclusion

The future of women’s soccer looks incredibly bright with rising stars like DeMelo, Fishel, Lee, Turnbow, and Thompson leading the charge. As they continue to hone their skills and gain experience, their presence in major tournaments will surely leave a lasting impression on fans and aspiring players. Keep your eyes peeled for these athletes as they strive for greatness in the coming years.
