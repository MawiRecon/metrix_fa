What’s the Deal?: Dave Franco’s New Projects – What to Watch Next

Published June 2025

Dave Franco, known for his versatile roles in both comedy and drama, has been capturing audiences' hearts with his charming performances and keen directorial vision. As we step into 2023, Franco is set to take on some intriguing new projects that you won't want to miss. Here’s a closer look at what to watch next in the world of Dave Franco.

Dave Franco’s New Projects – What to Watch Next

Upcoming Films: What to Expect

Franco has made a name for himself in movies ranging from teen comedies like Easy A to critically acclaimed dramas like Now You See Me. His foray into directing showcases his talents further, and with several projects lined up, fans can look forward to even more engaging content.

1. If We Don't Make It

One of Franco's most anticipated upcoming films is If We Don't Make It, which is set to debut in 2023. Directed by Franco himself, this film explores the complexities of modern relationships against the backdrop of a global crisis. The emotional depth and relevant themes are sure to resonate with audiences, making this a must-see film for fans and newcomers alike.

2. The Disaster Artist 2

Following the success of the original, Franco is also rumored to be working on a sequel to The Disaster Artist. Although details are sparse, the first film successfully highlighted the unique story behind the making of the cult classic The Room. Fans are eagerly waiting to see how Franco will continue this storytelling journey and inject his signature humor and insight into the sequel.

Exploring Diversity in Roles

Throughout his career, Dave Franco has shown a remarkable ability to switch between genres. His new projects exemplify this versatility, as he continues to take on diverse and challenging roles. From drama to comedy, not only does he entertain, but he also prompts thought and reflection in audiences.

Conclusion: Stay Tuned for More

As Dave Franco continues to evolve as an actor and director, audiences can look forward to a rich array of projects that promise to showcase his talent. Mark your calendars and prepare your watch lists, because there’s plenty of Franco content coming your way this year!
