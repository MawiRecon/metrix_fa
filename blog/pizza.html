What’s the deal?: Pizza

Published June 2025

Pizza is not just a dish; it’s a global phenomenon that has captured the hearts and appetites of millions. From casual family dinners to gourmet experiences, pizza comes in countless varieties and styles. In this blog post, we will explore the rich history of pizza, its various types, popular toppings, and even cover some controversial opinions surrounding this beloved food.

The Ultimate Guide to Pizza: History, Types, and Toppings

The Ultimate Guide to Pizza: History, Types, and Toppings

A Brief History of Pizza

The origins of pizza can be traced back to ancient civilizations. The Greeks and Egyptians both made flatbreads topped with various ingredients. However, it wasn't until the 18th century in Naples, Italy that pizza as we know it today was born. The classic Margherita pizza, featuring tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil, was created in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy in 1889, showcasing the colors of the Italian flag.

Types of Pizza

As pizza spread across the globe, different regions adapted it to their tastes. Below are some popular types of pizza:

  • Neapolitan Pizza: Characterized by its thin crust and simple toppings, Neapolitan pizza is often cooked in wood-fired ovens.
  • Chicago Deep-Dish Pizza: Known for its thick crust and layers of cheese and toppings, this style has a hearty appeal.
  • New York-Style Pizza: With its large, foldable slices and crispy crust, this pizza is a staple of New York City.
  • Sicilian Pizza: This square-shaped pizza has a thick crust and is typically topped with tomato sauce, onions, and anchovies.
  • California Pizza: Known for its innovative toppings, California pizza often incorporates unusual ingredients and fresh produce.

Popular Toppings

When it comes to pizza toppings, the possibilities are endless. While some traditional options include pepperoni, sausage, and mushrooms, there are many creative toppings to consider:

  • Pineapple
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives
  • Spinach
  • Goat cheese
  • Buffalo chicken

Controversial Opinion: Pineapple on Pizza

One of the most divisive debates in the culinary world is the controversial topic of pineapple on pizza. Some people argue that the sweetness of pineapple brings a unique contrast to the savory flavors of cheese and tomato sauce. Supporters of this topping claim it adds a refreshing twist to traditional pizza. On the other hand, purists believe that pineapple has no place on pizza, stating that it muddles the authentic Italian flavor profile. The debate sparks passion on both sides, and whether you love or hate this topping, it undeniably showcases the evolving nature of pizza.

Conclusion

From its humble beginnings in Naples to its status as a global favorite, pizza continues to be a source of joy and creativity for both chefs and diners alike. Whether you prefer a classic Margherita or a bold California-style creation, there’s a pizza out there for everyone. So the next time you’re craving a slice, remember the rich history and diversity that come with every bite!
