A Brief History of Pizza

Pizza has a storied history that dates back centuries. Originally a flatbread topped with various ingredients, it gained popularity in Naples, Italy, in the late 18th century. The classic Margherita pizza, topped with tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil, was named after Queen Margherita of Savoy and remains a timeless favorite.

Controversial Opinion: Pineapple on Pizza

Perhaps one of the most contentious debates in the pizza world is whether pineapple belongs on pizza. While some enthusiasts argue that the sweetness of pineapple perfectly complements the savory flavor of tomato and cheese, others consider it a culinary abomination. In my opinion, the latter group needs to embrace the wonderful combination of flavors that pineapple brings. After all, pizza is about innovation and creativity—why not experiment with unexpected toppings?
