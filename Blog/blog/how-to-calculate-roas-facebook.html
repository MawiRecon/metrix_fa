How to Calculate ROAS for Facebook Ads (And Why It Matters)

Published June 2025

Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) is one of the most important metrics in paid media — especially if you're running Facebook Ads on a budget. Knowing your ROAS helps you understand whether your campaigns are actually profitable.

To calculate ROAS, simply divide the revenue from your Facebook campaign by the total ad spend. For example, if you spent $200 and generated $1,000 in sales, your ROAS is 5.0 — meaning you made $5 for every $1 spent.

Most advertisers aim for at least a 3.0 ROAS on Facebook, but this number can vary widely by industry. What's important is tracking your numbers over time and identifying which audiences or creatives drive the best returns.
