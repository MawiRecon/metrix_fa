Why the Moon Might Be Hollow

Published June 2025

For years, conspiracy theorists have proposed that the Moon might not be a natural celestial body. Some believe it could be a hollowed-out structure or even an ancient alien observatory.

NASA's seismic studies show the Moon "rings like a bell" when struck by meteorites, which has fueled the hollow Moon theory — though most scientists dismiss it as nonsense.

Whether it's hollow or not, the Moon continues to capture imaginations around the world. And for website monetizers like you, it also creates the perfect excuse for a page full of ads.
